Features

PLANR supports your company in planning projects efficiently to best deliver results on time. PLANR's functions provide your management with a quick overview of the project portfolio and resource utilisation.
Operational planning

In this view, you create schedules for all your employees. First of all, the available resources are defined so that you always have the best overview. Then you continuously add employees and projects. Resources can easily be moved at any time and a possible rescheduling is very easily done. Your data is saved securely on an ongoing basis and automatically supplemented for you in the next planning rounds.

Capacity planning

You can switch from the first main view to the capacity planning of a work area at any time. The view by objects (project, service, absence) shows which employees are assigned to which objects in which time period. If a project or a service order is transferred from your order system to Planr, this line is automatically added and assigned to the available employees with a click.

Absence planning

With the absence planning function, the reason and duration of the absence can easily be made visible with different colours, so that you can see at any time which of your employees are available and when.
